Benchmark review
================

This page is for readers who need to review a TurboCell Atlas result rather than generate one.

How to review an output bundle
------------------------------

Read the bundle in this order:

#. the benchmark summary table
#. the query definitions
#. candidate-versus-rerank comparisons
#. the top-hit tables
#. the interpretation paragraph in the corresponding article

What to look for
----------------

The most important review question is not "did a method score well on average?" but "did the compressed method preserve the part of the neighborhood that matters for the stated biological question?"
